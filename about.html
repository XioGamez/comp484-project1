The Juicy Plot

Genre: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian Fiction

Summary

Darrow, a Red living on Mar, mining Helium-3, suffers a great loss that propels him into the hands of the Sons of Ares. He is coerced into attending the prestigious institute, with the idea that he can change the outcome. There Darrow must struggle to keep his secret and portray that he was always meant to be there. In enemy territory, will he be able to make it through his trials? Will he succeed or does his secret come to light?
